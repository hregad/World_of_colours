A colour atlas for GIS professionals

Curated palettes for cartographers and spatial analysts. Sequential, diverging, qualitative, categorical — each palette exports to QGIS and ArcGIS in one click. Click the map icon on any card to preview it on a world map.

World of Colours

A living atlas of GIS-ready colour palettes, maintained and expanded over time. Open source and free to use. Maintained by hregad on GitHub.

Export formats
.gpl — QGIS / GIMP
.clr — ArcGIS Color Ramp
.json — ArcGIS Pro CIM
.txt — Hex + RGB
