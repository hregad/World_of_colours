World of Colours

A living atlas of GIS colour palettes, organised by theme and updated regularly. Each palette is crafted to work in real cartographic contexts — sequential, diverging, qualitative, and categorical use cases. Hosted on GitHub. Open source, free to use.

Export formats
.gpl — QGIS / GIMP
.clr — ArcGIS color ramp
.json — ArcGIS Pro CIM
.txt — hex + RGB sheet
